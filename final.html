RECIPE FOR GULAB JAMUN

For the Gulab Jamun Balls:

  1. 1 cup of Khoya (milk solids)
  2. 2-3 tablespoons of all-purpose flour (maida)
  3. A pinch of baking soda (optional, but it helps in making them softer)
  4. 2-3 tablespoons of ghee (clarified butter)
  5. A few crushed cardamom pods (optional, for flavor)
  6. Chopped nuts like pistachios or almonds (optional, for garnish)
  7. Oil or ghee for deep frying

For the Sugar Syrup:

  1. 2 cups of granulated sugar
  2. 2 cups of water
  3. A few strands of saffron (optional)
  4. 1-2 teaspoons of rosewater or cardamom powder for flavoring
